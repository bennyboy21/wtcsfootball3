Who Are We
We are the WTCS (Western Technical-Commercial School) Colts Football Team. A Tier 1 team in the TDSB (Toronto District School Board). Our team is a diverse group of players from all over the GTA (Greater Toronto Area). We have had a team in the past, but with the passing of Peter Cram in 2012 the football team fell apart until in 2021 fall season where Russell Hoff the current head coach with the help of Stephen Espi worked hard to rebuild the team and played as a Varsity team during the 21-22 season.
